What is Study Subs?

Study Subs is a web application to help Japanese learners wean off of their subtitles dependencies.

How does it work?

Upload subtitle files (or any other file with Japanese text) on this page and you will be prompted to download all vocabulary words. If logged in, Study Subs keeps track of all vocabulary that you come across and allows you to download all previously seen vocabulary, all vocabulary in the uploaded subtitles file, or only previously unseen vocabulary in the subtitles file.

How do I study?

The downloaded file will be in CSV format, which is easily imported into an SRS study app such as Anki. This allows you to study the vocabulary from a TV show or movie so that the you are able to watch the movie without the distractions of subtitles.
